Garden Design & Creation – Your Garden, Reimagined and Realized

Do you dream of a garden that not only looks beautiful but also perfectly fits your lifestyle? At Lucky Shrub, we turn your vision into reality. Our garden design team works closely with you to understand your desires, your style, and the specifics of your property. From the initial sketch to detailed plant selection and materialization, we create custom concepts that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. Whether you want a lush retreat, a low-maintenance family garden, or a modern outdoor space, we design and create gardens where you truly feel at home.

Maintenance – Professional Care for Lasting Beauty

A beautiful garden needs regular care to preserve its splendor. With our comprehensive maintenance service, we take the work off your hands, ensuring your garden stays healthy and attractive year-round. Our experienced team handles everything – from precise pruning of your trees and shrubs to irrigation, fertilization, weed control, and pest management. We offer individualized care plans tailored to the specific needs of your plants and the size of your garden. This way, you can enjoy the beauty of your garden without worry, while we take care of the details.

Landscaping – More Than Just Plants, We Create Outdoor Spaces

At Lucky Shrub, landscaping goes far beyond mere planting. We view your outdoor area as an extension of your home and design it with foresight and expertise. Whether it's about laying paths and patios, building ponds or water features, installing lighting systems, or creating garden structures, our team masters the full spectrum of landscape construction. We harmoniously combine hard and soft elements to create functional, durable, and visually stunning landscapes that increase your property's value and bring you years of enjoyment.
